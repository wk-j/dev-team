## ğŸ¨ Understanding the Visual Metaphors

- Water flows from source to destination
- Work flows from idea to completion
- Multiple items can flow simultaneously
- Sometimes it rushes, sometimes it's calm

**Stream States**:
- **Nascent** = Just created, barely flowing
- **Flowing** = Normal healthy pace âœ…
- **Rushing** = High velocity, lots happening
- **Flooding** = TOO MUCH, needs help! ğŸš¨
- **Stagnant** = Blocked, nothing moving

Work items have **energy** that increases as you work:
- Starting = Low energy (dormant)
- Working = Building energy (kindling â†’ blazing)
- Completing = Crystallizing energy into permanent form

### Why "Crystals"?
Completed work **crystallizes** into permanent gems:
- Can't be undone (preserved achievements)
- Have **facets** (quality/complexity)
- Have **brilliance** (value/impact)
- Displayed in a garden (trophy case)

### Why "Resonance"?
Team collaboration creates **resonance** (connection strength):
- Like musical instruments in harmony
- Grows through working together
- Shows as glowing lines between members
- Higher resonance = better teamwork
