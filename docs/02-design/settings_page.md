## Sanctum Appearance

### Theme Dimensions
Your Sanctum can have different environmental themes:

| Theme | Description | Mood |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Deep Void** | Default dark space | Focused, calm |
| **Nebula Garden** | Colorful gas clouds | Creative, expansive |
| **Crystal Cave** | Reflective crystalline | Achievement-focused |
| **Ocean Depths** | Bioluminescent underwater | Flowing, peaceful |
| **Aurora Fields** | Northern lights on dark plains | Inspiring, dynamic |

### Custom Sanctum Elements
- **Background**: Choose your dimension
- **Ambient Particles**: Style and density
- **Central Object**: Your celestial body styling
- **Accent Lighting**: Color temperature and intensity
- **Sound**: Ambient audio for your space

## Exiting the Sanctum

When you leave:
1. Changes save automatically
2. Sanctum fades with gentle dissolve
3. You phase back into Observatory
4. Your new settings take effect immediately
5. Brief "Settings synced" confirmation appears
