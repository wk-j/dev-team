## Adding New Members

### The Birth of a Star
When someone joins the team:
1. A **nebula cloud** appears at edge of system
2. Cloud coalesces over onboarding period
3. New star ignites with small celebration
4. Mentor connection forms immediately
5. Star gradually finds its natural orbit

### Ritual:
- Team sees "New star forming in [Team] system"
- Existing members can send "welcome resonance" pings
- First week: Star has protective nebula (learning mode)

---

## Departure Ceremony

### When a Member Leaves
Instead of abrupt removal:
1. Star announces "entering final orbit"
2. Knowledge transfer connections strengthen
3. Star gradually dims over transition period
4. Finally transforms into a **memorial comet**
5. Comet occasionally passes through system (alumni energy)

### Memorial Comets
- Named after departed members
- Carry their contribution history
- Pass through occasionally as reminder
- Click to see their legacy
